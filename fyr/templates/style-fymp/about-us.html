WriteToThem.com -

We would love to tell you that we're a terrifyingly well-organised e-democracy strike force, whose members have years of lobbying experience fighting for justice in the corridors of Westminster. But we'd be lying. Again.

In reality, we are a dozen or so volunteers who don't see why ordinary people should have to jump through hoops to contact their elected MP.

We do this in our spare time, because we think it's worth doing. And if it's worth doing, we might as well try to do it well. We've recently helped to launch TheyWorkForYou.com, a new website which lets you keep track of what your MP does and says in Parliament.

Most people don't know the name of their MP, let alone their constituency. We think it's important that constituents know the identity of their Parliamentary representative, and be able to contact him or her when they feel the need.

Hence this website.

You can read what the press has written about us here. We've won the odd award, but it's feedback from our users which gives us the biggest buzz.
