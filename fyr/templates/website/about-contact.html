If you are an elected representative

If you are an elected representative wishing to report an abusive message or change in contact details, please email team@writetothem.com for prompt action. If you do not have access to email, you may send a fax or leave a voicemail on 07031 150 115. Please note: this number is for elected representatives only to leave a message for mySociety. Do not use this number to try and contact ether a representative or a constituent; those messages will not be passed on.
