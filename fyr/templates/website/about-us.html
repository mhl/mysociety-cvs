The predecessor to this site, FaxYourMP.com, was built by a group of volunteers who thought that it would be a good thing if it was easier for people to contact their MPs.

mySociety is a charitable organisation which has grown out of this community of volunteers. Our first project was to take FaxYourMP and rebuild it bigger and better. mySociety's primary mission is to build internet projects which give people simple, tangible benefits in the civic and community aspects of their lives. This is the FaxYourMP ethic, and mySociety's job is to extend it via a series of new sites. WriteToThem is the first of five projects we're building in 2005.

We got some funding to help do this from the Office of the Deputy Prime Minister's e-innovations fund, in partnership with West Sussex County Council. Many thanks to them.
