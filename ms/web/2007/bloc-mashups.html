A. Bed and Breakfast map

  • On basic B&B site show people where it is

  • Change the latitude and longitude - find new one using search on Google Maps, then use "Link to this page" and look in the URL.

Resources

mySociety sites

  • TheyWorkForYou. Postcode lookup of Welsh Constituencies. Find informatoin about Welsh MPs. You can add the Welsh Assembly.
  • Planning Alerts. Made by Richard Pope, as a volunteer. Denbighshire County Council only. You can add more councils in Wales to it.
  • Neighbourhood FixIt. Report broken things to Welsh councils.
  • PledgeBank. Make pledges, can be specific to an area. Postcode / town lookup of active pledges. You can finish off the Welsh translation.
